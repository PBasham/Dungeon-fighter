Instructions

Welcome to my game!, Your goal is to traverse a cave and rescue your friend that is being held captive by the Orcs!

How to play

You (the teal colored shape)will use W-"Up", A-"Left", S-"Down", D-"Right" to move around until you engage with an enemy (the green tinted shapes)by walking over them. Once in a fight, the fight buttons at the bottom left corner of the screen will turn active and the round wills start off with the enemy showing you their intent! This means they will show you if they are going to attack or defend. You will have to decide each turn if it's better for you to respond with attacking or defending by using the mouse to select one of the fight buttons. The battle ends if you or your enemy's health reaches zero! You can use any items you collect at the bottom right corner by clicking it with your mouse. If your health reaches zero, this results in a game over!

Win condition

To win, you need to defeat any Orcs in your way and reach your friend(the yellow shape)! Once you reach them, you win!
